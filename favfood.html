Homemade Samosas (Nepali Taste)

Aloo samosa is one of the most popular and favorite snack in Nepal. This populat street food 'samosa' is mouth-watering and addictive.

Total time: 1 hour approximately

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Potatoes and Boiled peas
  • Roasted Peanuts
  • Green chillies and corrriander
  • Grated ginger
  • Roasted cummin
  • Garam Masala
  • Salt and Oil

Preparation

  1. Start by making the samosa dough. To a large bowl, add flour, ajwain, salt and mix well.

    Time: 15 minutes

  2. Boil potatoes until done. Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a pan on medium heat. Once the oil is hot, add the cumin seeds, fennel seeds and crushed coriander seeds.

    Time: 15 minutes

  3. Mix everything together. I use my potato masher to combine the spices and seasonings well with the potatoes and peas. Mix to combine. Once it’s all well incorporated, remove pan from heat and let the filling cool down a bit.

    Time: 10 minute

  4. BRoll one dough ball into a circle-oval kind of shape, around 6-7 inches in diameter and then cut it into two parts. Fill the samosa with the potato filling, around 1 to 2 tablespoons. Don’t overfill the samosa.

    Time: 10 minutes
